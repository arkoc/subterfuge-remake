# Specialist Combos — Community Meta (Subterfuge 2015)

> Research notes on what *Subterfuge* players actually used to win games in the 2015–2017 era, mined from the official forums (`forums.subterfuge-game.com`), the Fandom wiki, the *Designing Subterfuge* dev blog, and assorted strategy posts. The forum is intermittently offline (often hard 403/connection-refused), so several thread URLs are cited with content reconstructed from Google's index excerpts — those entries are marked `[partial source]`.
>
> Our current sim only has 10 of the ~25 specialists shipped in the real game (King, Queen, Pirate, Inspector, Sentry, Foreman, Tinkerer, Double Agent, Helmsman, and Princess/Saboteur in the next-up pools). Many cited combos involve specialists we have not yet built — they are still listed because they reveal *interaction shapes* we should keep room for.

---

## 1. Top 10 community meta combos (ranked)

Ranking is by frequency of citation across the wiki's "Specialist Combos" page, the "Cool Combos", "Specialist Combo Strategies", "Ranking specialists", and "Domination specialist strategies" forum threads, and the "please NERF the king" balance thread. "Tier" is the consensus from those threads, not a quantitative metric.

### #1 — King-stacking (a.k.a. "the King meta")
- **Recipe:** Hypnotist → King, repeated. Plus enough Security Chiefs / Queen to cancel the global shield penalty.
- **Why it's S-tier:** A single King already converts a 3:1 driller advantage into free kills across **every combat you participate in, globally**. Multiple Kings were the dominant late-game force; the Fandom wiki notes "shield effects stack but the King's offensive global does **not** stack" — but the perception that *more Kings = more wins* persisted because each King also gives +20 shield at its outpost while the global −20 shield is fixed per side.
- **Citation:** "please NERF the king" thread documents 8 Kings in a TET final and 3 Kings in the Holiday Tournament final dominating decisively. `[partial source]` http://forums.subterfuge-game.com/viewtopic.php?f=5&t=1463 ; King page https://subterfuge.fandom.com/wiki/King ; King combat-stacking patch thread `[partial source]` http://forums.subterfuge-game.com/viewtopic.php?f=5&t=2674
- **Dev response:** "King global combat bonus no longer stacks" was eventually patched in. The shield half kept stacking.

### #2 — "The Queen's Bounty" (Queen-hunting)
- **Recipe:** Pirate + Navigator + Assassin on one sub.
- **How it works:** Pirate doubles speed and locks onto a target sub; if the Queen flees, Navigator redirects; on contact, Assassin (combat priority 6) kills the Queen regardless of combat outcome. Forum and wiki call this "the ultimate Queen hunting combo".
- **Counter:** Revered Elder on the Queen's sub blocks specialist phase (the only space-efficient counter; Bounty has no Elder slot). Otherwise: keep Queen at a deep-interior outpost behind sonar nets.
- **Citation:** https://subterfuge.fandom.com/wiki/Specialist_Combos ; community Q&A "Is there any way to counter the 'queen's bounty' trio?" https://subterfuge.fandom.com/f/p/3147298464858349675

### #3 — "Impenetrable Outpost" (Queen + King + Security Chief)
- **Recipe:** Queen (+20 local shield), King (+20 local), Security Chief (+10 local + 10 global). On a Generator outpost, that's roughly a 60+ shield-max fortress.
- **Why it's S-tier:** With an Inspector also parked here, shields are instantly fully recharged on arrival of any defending sub and after combat. The wiki's exact phrasing on the (now-renamed) "Queen + Dictator + Inspection Agent" variant: "an outpost with 20 shields … will end up with 80 fully charged shields and a hefty combat bonus for any drillers present."
- **Citation:** https://subterfuge.fandom.com/wiki/Specialist_Combos ; https://subterfuge.fandom.com/wiki/Security_Chief

### #4 — Thief + Engineer ("snowball combat")
- **Recipe:** Thief (CP 4, converts 15% of enemy drillers) + Engineer (repairs 25–50% after victorious combat). Strengthens to "you literally gain drillers per fight" with Thief + Engineer + Engineer.
- **Why strong:** Wiki quote: "with Thief + Engineer + Engineer you practically gain units after every combat, as long as you have a bit more than 2/3 of the enemy's drillers."
- **Citation:** https://subterfuge.fandom.com/wiki/Specialist_Combos

### #5 — Pirate + Navigator combo strategy
- **Recipe:** Pirate (2× speed targeting a sub, 4× return speed) + Navigator (redirect mid-flight) ± Helmsman / Lieutenant.
- **Why strong:** Catches anything; the *Domination* thread classifies "Pirate-Nav" as a top-tier strategy bucket alongside General-stacking and Admiral/Tycoon-stacking. "Strong against general stacking and mega sentry; weak against Tycoon and Admiral stacking."
- **Citation:** http://forums.subterfuge-game.com/viewtopic.php?f=5&t=2962 `[partial source]` (Domination specialist strategies)

### #6 — Smuggler + Navigator ("the fakeout")
- **Recipe:** Smuggler aims a sub at your own outpost → travels at 3× (the friendly-bound speed bonus) → Navigator redirects to enemy outpost at the *last moment*.
- **Why strong:** Effectively grants 3× speed on an attack; "unstoppable with a Revered Elder aboard" per the wiki.
- **Citation:** https://subterfuge.fandom.com/wiki/Specialist_Combos

### #7 — "Smuggler Cheese" (Foreman shuffle)
- **Recipe:** Smuggler ferries a Foreman between two Factories so the Foreman's +6 drillers/cycle effectively doubles up across two production sites.
- **Citation:** https://subterfuge.fandom.com/wiki/Foreman `[partial source]`

### #8 — "Dancing Martyr"
- **Recipe:** Martyr + Navigator (+ optional Helmsman / Smuggler).
- **How it works:** Navigator allows feinting between targets so the defender can't pre-empt the blast destination. Martyr's blast radius destroys subs *and* outposts within 20% of standard sonar range.
- **Citation:** https://subterfuge.fandom.com/wiki/Specialist_Combos

### #9 — "Triple Agent"
- **Recipe:** Double Agent + Speed unit (ideally Admiral, fallback Helmsman/Lieutenant). Send Double Agent at enemy → it swaps to them → chase with a 1-man speed sub → re-trigger Double Agent → he comes back to your side.
- **Citation:** http://forums.subterfuge-game.com/viewtopic.php?f=5&t=1022 `[partial source]` "[Tactic] [Specialist Combo] Triple Agent"

### #10 — General + Diplomat (specialist-spam wear-down)
- **Recipe:** Defender holds a General (kills 10 enemy drillers in every spec-phase combat globally) + Diplomat (auto-releases your captives back). Attacker can keep sending lone specialists at the outpost; General chews drillers each time, Diplomat re-cycles your specialist back.
- **Citation:** https://subterfuge.fandom.com/wiki/Specialist_Combos

Honorable mentions: **Sentry + Princess** (Princess's +50% sonar effectively doubles Sentry's killing range — Sentry range is half sonar), **Saboteur + Diplomat** ("Endless defense" — the Saboteur returns to keep redirecting), **Saboteur + General** (Saboteur redirects, General shreds drillers), **Inspector + Smuggler + Security Chief on one sub** ("fast moving 20-shield bubble that instantly recharges"), **Booming** = Tinkerer + Minister of Energy stack to lift driller cap.

## 2. Sub-vs-sub combat combo notes

Combat-point (CP) order matters because earlier-priority specialists resolve before later ones can react:

| CP | Specialist     | Effect                                                                 |
|----|----------------|------------------------------------------------------------------------|
| 1  | Martyr         | Wipes everything in 20%-sonar blast                                    |
| 2  | Revered Elder  | Cancels all specialists unless other side also has one                 |
| 3  | Saboteur       | Redirects one enemy sub to its owner's nearest outpost                 |
| 4  | Infiltrator    | Drains attacked outpost's shields                                      |
| 4  | Thief          | 15% of enemy drillers to your side                                     |
| 5  | Double Agent   | Both subs lose drillers, swap ownership, combat ends                   |
| 6  | Assassin       | Kills all enemy specialists present                                    |
| 7  | Lieutenant/War Hero | Kill 5 / 20 enemy drillers                                        |

Patterns observed in community play:

- **Assassin (CP 6) vs Double Agent (CP 5):** Double Agent fires first, swaps the sub, *then* combat ends — Assassin never resolves. Wiki explicitly notes this. https://subterfuge.fandom.com/wiki/Assassin
- **Revered Elder** is the universal "cancel" — used as the *only* clean counter to a Queen's Bounty because Bounty has no Elder slot.
- **Saboteur weakness:** redirects only one sub. Multi-sub swarms render him useless; couple with General or War Hero to keep the redirected sub-stream still bleeding.
- **Pirate-on-Pirate / Pirate-on-Helmsman speed math:** Helmsman is 2× base; Pirate is 2× while pursuing, 4× returning. A defending Helmsman can outrun a non-Pirate chaser but loses straight-line races to a pursuing Pirate. Speed-stacking notes in the "Pirate + Navigator" wiki section.

## 3. Outpost defense combo notes

Community-favoured fortress recipes (all from Specialist Combos page + the King/Security Chief wiki entries):

- **"Brick"**: Queen + King + Security Chief (+ Inspector for instant recharge) → ~60+ max shield, full recharge after each combat, +1/3 driller kill ratio from King.
- **"Sonar trap"**: Princess + Sentry (Sentry range scales with sonar; Princess's +50% sonar doubles effective kill area) + Intelligence Officer for further +25%.
- **"Refilling shield"**: Inspector parked on a Generator. Every defending sub arriving and every post-combat resolution instantly tops up the shield. Combined with Saboteur defender it gates one attacker per cycle.
- **"Booming"**: Tinkerer + Minister of Energy (+ Tycoon) at a fortress outpost. Tinkerer drains the shield to *give you* +3 × max shield in electrical output. With high max shield and the cap raised by Minister of Energy, you can sustain a much larger garrison. This is the only meta justification for Tinkerer.

## 4. Counter-strategy patterns

The community's strategy "rock-paper-scissors" (per the *Domination specialist strategies* forum thread `[partial source]` http://forums.subterfuge-game.com/viewtopic.php?f=5&t=2962):

- **General-stacking** beats **Admiral-stacking / Tycoon-stacking** (driller deletion outpaces production).
- **Pirate-Navigator** beats **General-stacking** and **Mega-Sentry** (you can fly past their pings and shred their key specialists before Generals enter combat).
- **Admiral/Tycoon stacking** beats **Pirate-Navigator** (you outproduce the harassment).
- **Mega-Sentry** (carpet the map with Sentries via Princesses) beats none of the above cleanly — described as "you might see it once in a while but it takes too long to build up".
- **Revered Elder** is the universal counter to specialist combos; you sacrifice your own combat specialists by carrying him.
- **Diplomat** counters **Hypnotist/King capture** (Diplomat takes priority over Hypnotist when a specialist is captured within sonar — they immediately return to your side).
- **Saboteur** to take down shields: actually rare; the community's "shield drain" counter to a fortress was usually **Infiltrator** (CP 4 specialist that drains the attacked outpost's shield) plus driller mass, not Saboteur (Saboteur redirects subs, doesn't drop shields).

## 6. Implications for our rebuild

### 6a. Combos to explicitly *test for* in the sim

Each of these should become a deterministic vitest case under `packages/sim/test/`:

1. **King-stacking ceiling.** With 2 Kings on the same side in combat: confirm the *global combat ability* does **not** stack but the *shield deltas* sum (Kings 1 & 2 both apply +20 local at their own outpost; the −20 global penalty applies once per King). Mirror the patched behaviour ("King global combat bonus no longer stacks").
2. **Queen's Bounty intercept.** Scripted scenario: Queen flees in a sub, Pirate+Navigator+Assassin chases. Verify (a) Pirate's 2× pursuit speed, (b) Navigator redirect resolves before contact, (c) Assassin's CP-6 kill fires even on a tied combat. Revered Elder on the Queen sub should null this — second test case.
3. **Brick outpost shield math.** Queen + King + Security Chief at a 20-shield Generator: assert max shield = 60 and `tickRecharge` reaches full after each combat when Inspector is present.
4. **Thief + Engineer snowball.** Two-side combat with attacker carrying Thief + 2× Engineer: assert net drillers ≥ pre-combat as long as attacker had > 2/3 of defender's drillers. This is a strong invariant we can lock in.
5. **CP-order edge case: Double Agent before Assassin.** Both subs carry both specialists; assert Double Agent (CP 5) resolves first, swaps ownership, and Assassin (CP 6) does not run.
6. **Saboteur weakness vs swarm.** One Saboteur defender, three incoming attacker subs. Assert Saboteur redirects exactly one and the other two land.
7. **Inspector recharge cadence.** Inspector parked on outpost: shield must be `max` immediately after every `resolveCombat` call that occurred at that outpost, even if the tick fell mid-recharge.
8. **Triple Agent loop.** Send our Double Agent → swaps to enemy → send a speed sub at the swapped Double Agent → it swaps back. Confirm two ownership swaps in `< sub-base-travel-time * 1.5`.
9. **Smuggler / Navigator speed override.** Smuggler aimed at friendly outpost (3× speed) then redirected mid-flight to enemy: verify the 3× speed persists for the full leg or behaves per docs (this is a *gameplay* decision we have to make explicit in `docs/02_subs.md`).
10. **Dancing Martyr blast radius.** Martyr with Navigator: verify blast radius == 20% of standard sonar, applies to both subs and outposts (we have no outpost-destroy primitive yet — flagged).

### 6b. Combos that need balance attention before they ship

- **King stacking.** The community burned a year on this; we should *ship* with global combat bonus already non-stacking. Patch from 2016 lives in `viewtopic.php?f=5&t=2674`.
- **Queen's Bounty.** Either we keep the *Designing Subterfuge* "abilities interact with systems, not specialists" stance and accept Bounty as intended, or we deliberately add an interception delay between Navigator redirect and Pirate target re-acquisition. Recommend: **ship as designed**, the community considered Revered Elder a sufficient counter.
- **Tinkerer.** Marginal in the original meta. Worth verifying the formula (`+3 × max-shield to electrical output, drains 3 shield/hour`) once we add the electrical / driller-cap subsystem (docs/03), otherwise he's strictly worse than other rare-pool picks.
- **Mega-Sentry.** Sentry range = ½ sonar in the original. We need to confirm our sonar model matches *before* the Princess-stacking interaction goes in (otherwise Sentry kill area scales wrong).
- **Double Agent + Speed:** "Triple Agent" is a clean exploit-style loop. Worth a design decision: do we want it? Original game shipped it.

### 6c. Specialists we have but haven't yet stress-tested for combos

From the 10 we currently model, the **Foreman** has the most under-developed interaction surface in our sim (no `Foreman + speed-courier` test, no production-cycle integration test). The **Tinkerer**'s electrical interaction depends on subsystems we haven't built yet (electrical cap → driller cap from docs/03). The **Inspector** in our code only restores on arrival, not "after every combat at the outpost while present" — that's the community's main use case and we are currently shipping a weaker version.
